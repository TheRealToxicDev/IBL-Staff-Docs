The Infinity Management Team is the base of our Staff Team
their job is to Manage and Maintain the Infinity Staff
and ensure all rules and regulations are being followed.

---

## Task List

| Task        | Description |
| ----------- | ----------- |
| 1.          | Help the Staff Team where and when needed with Tickets, Approval, Support, Reports etc. |
| 2.          | Help members with issues and support and direct them to the Owners or Dev Team when absolutely necessary. |
| 3.          | Interact with our members and do your part in keeping our community safe and happy |
